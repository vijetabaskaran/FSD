About Loki

Loki Laufeyson is a fictional character appearing in Marvel Comics, inspired by the Norse mythological deity of the same name. Created by Stan Lee, Larry Lieber, and Jack Kirby, Loki is known as the God of Mischief and is one of the most complex characters in the Marvel Universe. He possesses extraordinary magical abilities, intelligence, and a talent for deception, making him both a formidable adversary and an intriguing anti-hero.
